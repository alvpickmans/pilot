Iconography & Symbol Library
============================

- Industrial pictograms: gear, bolt, crane, shield, valve, container, lab flask, document
- Encoding-style shapes: bars, dots, modular patterns to imply data/readout and process
- Atomic-science symbols: nucleus, orbitals, electron paths
- Unit/department icons: IT, QA, HR, Lab, Shipping, Admin
- Status and clearance badges: stripes, ticks, and border badges

Construction Rules
- Stroke weight: 2 px (consistent across icons)
- Alignment: icons centered; maintain square bounding boxes when possible
- Geometry: geometric forms; avoid rounded corners unless appropriate for the symbol
- Coordinate origin: top-left at 0,0; 24x24 or 32x32 viewBoxes are recommended for consistency
