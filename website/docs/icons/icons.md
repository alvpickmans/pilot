title: Iconography & Symbols
---

- Industrial pictograms: gear, bolt, crane, shield, valve, container, lab flask, document
- Encoding shapes: bars, dots, modular patterns
- Atomic symbols: nucleus, orbitals
- Unit icons: IT, QA, Lab, Shipping, Admin
- Status badges: stripes, ticks

Construction rules
- Stroke weight: 2 px; aligned to 8 px grid
- Bounding boxes: square when possible; centered
- Geometry: geometric shapes; avoid unnecessary curves
